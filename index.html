How to Play

KPI Hangman — Katalys Accounting

🎯 Objective
🏆 Be the first player to correctly guess the hidden KPI phrase — one letter at a time or by guessing the whole phrase at once.
👤 Getting Started
1️⃣ Enter your name in the Player Portal at the bottom of the screen, then click Join to enter the game.
2️⃣ Check in daily by completing the KPI task shown on the left panel. Your KPI response unlocks your keyboard for that session.
🔤 Making Guesses
🔡 Letter guesses: Click any letter on the keyboard to guess it. Correct letters are revealed in green; wrong letters are shown in red and cost you a life.
💬 Phrase guess: Type the full phrase in the text box and press Guess Phrase. A wrong phrase guess also costs a life — use wisely!
❤️ Lives
💚 Each player starts with 6 lives, shown as green dots next to your name. Every wrong guess costs one life.
💀 Lose all 6 lives and you're eliminated — you can still watch but can't make more guesses.
🏅 Winning
🎉 The player who correctly guesses the full phrase wins the round. A new puzzle starts the next cycle!
🔄 Once a puzzle is won, the admin will set a new phrase. New puzzle, new chance to win!
💡 Pro tip: Complete your KPI check-in early each day — it unlocks your keyboard so you're ready to guess when it's your turn. Look for common short words like AND, THE, OF to crack the phrase faster!
